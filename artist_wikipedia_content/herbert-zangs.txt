

Herbert Zangs (27 March 1924 – 26 March 2003) was a German artist.

Zangs was born and died in Krefeld. He studied at the Kunstakademie Düsseldorf together with Günter Grass. In 1960 he won the Vincent van Volkmer Prize.[1]

There are several books about Zangs in the German national Library (dnb).[2]
