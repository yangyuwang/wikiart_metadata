From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Norwegian artist

Camilla Løw (born 1976) is a contemporary Norwegian artist. She graduated from Asker Kunstskole in Norway in 1998. After this, she left for Glasgow, where she graduated from the Glasgow School of Art in 2001 with a BA in Fine Art.[1]

Exhibitions

[edit]
  • 2008 Straight Letters, Pier Arts Centre, Orkney[2]
  • 2008 Straight Letters, Dundee Contemporary Arts, Dundee[3]
  • 2008 Embraced Open Reassembled, Sutton Lane, London[4]
  • 2008 New Ruins, Bergen Kunsthall No.5[5]
  • 2010 Culture & Leisure, New Art Centre, Roche Court, Salisbury[6]
  • 2012 The Space of Shape-Time, The National Museum of Art, Architecture and Design, Oslo[7]
  • 2013 Camilla Løw:One Night Only, UKS, Oslo[8]
  • 2016 Eye in the Sky, Kunstnerforbundet, Oslo[9]
