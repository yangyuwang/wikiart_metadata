Georges Lemmen
Born25 November 1865
Schaerbeek, Belgium
Died5 June or 5 July 1916
Brussels, Belgium
NationalityBelgian
Known forpainting
Notable workThe Beach at Heist, Aline Marechal, Vase of Flowers
MovementNeo-impressionism, Post-Impressionism, modern art

Georges Lemmen (1865, Schaerbeek – 1916, Brussels) was a neo-impressionist painter from Belgium. He was a member of Les XX from 1888.[1] His works include The Beach at Heist, Aline Marechal and Vase of Flowers. Yvonne Serruys studied in his workshop in Brussels from 1892 to 1894.
