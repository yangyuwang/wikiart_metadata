Born(1929-08-05)5 August 1929

Died16 March 2021(2021-03-16) (aged 91)
Milan, Italy
NationalityItalian
Known forPainting
Websitearchivioturisimeti.it

Turi Simeti (5 August 1929 – 16 March 2021) was an Italian painter born in Alcamo, Sicily, Italy.

In 2014 he received the "Artist of the year" prize of the Circolo della Stampa of Milan.[1]
